# Lansing Trade Group (lansing-trade-group)

Lansing Trade Group was an international merchandiser, marketer, and logistics company specializing in bulk commodities including grains, biofuels, and energy products. In 2022, The Andersons completed the acquisition of Lansing Trade Group; its commodities business now operates as part of The Andersons' Trade Group.
